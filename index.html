The Battle of Targoviste

Riley of Fire and Blood

After spending Fifteen years growing up in refuge in the Wildlands during the purging of Mages in Targoviste, by the order of Bishop Axel of the Catholic Church. Riley of Fire and Blood, now a level 20 Mage is back seeking revenge and her lost brothers. Instead, she meets a ravaged city destroyed by Mehmed II. Mehmed II and his henchmen have waged war on the people of Targoviste and Vlad Tepes (Dracula), the natives left alive attempt to flee to neighbouring cities. Riley discovers an undergound network of new genaration mages who intend to go to war with Mehmed II and his hoard and therefore seek her help, she finds the new genaration of Mages are helping Dracula and the Catholic church fight off the nightly raid of Targoviste which greatly displeases her. She later finds a now repentant Axel and attempts to kill him but fails to, this act angers the mages and forces them to reject Riley as one of them, eventually pushing her away to join Mehmed II and his hoard.

Weaknesses

There are two main ways to weaken Riley.

  1. The blood witch casts powerful magic which reduces her stamina greatly, Engage her with continuous long range attacts to tire her out and diminish her stamina
  2. She's can't invoke a lot of magic without her spell book. Steal or Destroy her spellbook and you have a great chance of defeating her

Traits

A few things to note about The blood witch

  • She casts Long and Short range fire Magic.
  • The longer the range the weaker the attacks
  • Riley has the ability to manipulate anyone whose blood she touches
    so you should avoid bleeding in Battle
  • She also has the ability to cast illusions from short ranges
  • She tends to get her own way
  • She is loyal to no one
  • Enjoys her own Company
